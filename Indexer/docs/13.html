  • Expand stub pages to start page assessment
  • Ensure that every article is cited; no article should be without at least one reference

Guidelines[edit]

Motion Articles[edit]

  • [lead paragraph - briefly defining motion]
  • Explanation and Use
    • Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised (RONR)
    • The Standard Code (TSC)
    • [Other Parliamentary Authorities (PA)]
    • Legislative Use
  • History
  • Related Motions and Concepts

PA Citations[edit]

  • Use these abbreviations only after the full title has been included on the page
    • RONR - Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised
    • TSC - The Standard Code of Parliamentary Procedure
  • Demeter's Manual (1969) is the 'Blue Book' edition.
  • DEM, MAS, RID are abbreviations used in Template:Cite parl only, not in article text
