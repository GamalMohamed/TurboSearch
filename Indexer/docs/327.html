The following is a directory of portals in the English Wikipedia, along with who founded them and when, and who maintains them.

At present, there are 173 featured portals, of a total of 1476 portals on Wikipedia.

Please insert new entries under the appropriate category. Please list your portal using {{Lp}} (i.e. {{Lp|Name}}). When the portal is complete and of good quality, add it to Portal:Contents/Portals. Please note that if portals added to these pages do not conform with the portal guidelines, then they will be removed.
